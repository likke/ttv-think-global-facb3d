About Fleire

Fleire Castro is a Filipino founder, AI marketing systems builder, trainer, and technology-for-social-good advocate. She started her career as a software developer, built a digital marketing company, and went on to create technology ventures serving businesses across multiple markets.

Today, she helps organizations move from AI curiosity to useful, responsible adoption. Her approach is direct: teach the fundamentals, build around real problems, protect human judgment, and make every learning experience lead to action.

Based in the Philippines · Available for local, regional & international engagements
